OS_Off, switch machine off

RST 20H, DEFW $EC06

IN: 

     -
OUT: 
     -
Registers changed after return: 
     A.BCDEHL/IXIY same
     .F....../.... different
Notes:
This switches the machine off - the equivalent of pressing both <SHIFT> keys down.
